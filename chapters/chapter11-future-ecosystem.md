### Long-term vision

#### MML as global infrastructure
In a world where technologies evolve rapidly but crises multiply, MML represents an **information invariant** - a guarantee that essential information can always circulate, be understood and be acted upon, whatever the surrounding technological conditions.

#### Durable legacy
MML is not just a technical format. It's a **humanistic communication philosophy** that places accessibility, resilience and universality at the heart of information exchange. In a century, when our current technologies will be obsolete, MML will still be able to transmit the vital information that saves lives and preserves human knowledge.

**Chapter conclusion**: The future MML ecosystem extends far beyond a simple data format.
